General Instructions for Gameplay:

There are two characters
Player one has WASD, Z, X, and C as their controls
Player two has Arrow keys, ",", ".", ad "/" as controls

WASD and Arrow keys are for movement
Z and "," are for blocking (reducing damage)
X ad "." are for normal attacks
C and "/" are for heavy attacks

PS4 Controller: Joystick to move up, down, left right. Addition controls: L1, L2 and R2 for blocking, normal attacks, and heavy attacks.
